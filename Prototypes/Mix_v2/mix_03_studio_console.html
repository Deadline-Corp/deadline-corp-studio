// how we work

No discovery theatre.
We move fast.

Four phases, compressed. We've done this enough times to know where the real friction lives — and it's not where most agencies charge for it.

01

Scope call · 60 min

We ask the hard questions up front: what exists, what breaks, what actually needs to change. No NDA theatre on day one.

02

Technical spec · 3 days

Architecture decision records, stack rationale, risk surface. Written by the engineer who will build it, not by a PM who won't.

03

Build in weekly milestones

You get a staging deploy every Friday. Async Loom updates, shared linear board, no status-meeting overhead unless you want it.

04

Handover with runbooks

Full documentation, incident runbooks, monitoring setup. Your team should be able to own this the day we hand it over.
