how we work

Не торопимся.

01

We begin by listening. Before the first line of code, we spend time understanding the problem as it actually exists — not the brief version, not the optimistic version, but the version with all its constraints and contradictions. This phase takes as long as it takes.

02

We write a specification. Not a proposal, not a feature list — a document that describes the system's intended behaviour in plain language. You can read it, question it, and sign off on it. We do not start building until this document is settled.

03

We build in short, demonstrable increments. Every week, you see working software. Not screenshots, not mockups — the actual thing, running. You give us feedback in the context of real use, not imagination. This produces better outcomes than long silences punctuated by big reveals.

04

We hand over ownership. Documentation, source code, infrastructure access — everything that belongs to you is yours, fully and without caveat. We do not build dependencies on our tools or our goodwill. The relationship continues because it is valuable, not because you are locked in.
