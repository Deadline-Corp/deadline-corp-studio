How we work

The process

01

Listen before defining

We start with one conversation, and we listen for what isn't said. The scope document that comes after is short — three pages, no more. It describes what we're building and, more importantly, what we're not.

02

Build in the open

Weekly working demos, not progress reports. You see the thing running, you give us your reaction, and we adjust. No surprises at handover. No big-bang reveals that leave no room for honest feedback.

03

Finish properly

Deployment is not the end. We stay for a week after launch — watching logs, fixing edge cases that only appear in production, answering questions from real users. Не торопимся. We are not in a hurry.

04

Return when needed

We keep working relationships, not support tickets. If something needs changing in six months, you write to us and we reply. No contracts to re-negotiate. We remember the project because we cared about it.
