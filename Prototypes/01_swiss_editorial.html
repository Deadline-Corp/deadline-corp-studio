We are not a vendor. We do not sell templates or run generic Wordpress installs. We study the business first — the economics, the conversion points, the bottlenecks — and then we build tools that directly address those problems. 03 — Process

How we move from first call to shipped product.

01

Discovery

2 – 3 days

We interview stakeholders, review analytics, and map the revenue model. The output is a one-page brief: what we build, why, and what success looks like numerically. If we cannot agree on that brief, we do not proceed.

02

Architecture

3 – 5 days

System design, stack selection, data model, integration map. We choose the simplest architecture that meets the requirements — not the most impressive one. Complexity is a cost, not a feature.

03

Sprint Build

1 – 4 weeks

Execution in focused sprints with weekly check-ins. Working software at the end of every sprint — no month-long silences followed by a demo. You see progress continuously.

04

Handoff

2 – 3 days

Documentation, runbooks, recorded walkthroughs. Your team owns it completely. We remain available for questions for 30 days post-launch at no additional charge.
